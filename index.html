Gear up for Space Gunner! Blast enemies, climb the leaderboard, and prove you're the top space ace. Register now to track your progress and dominate the galaxy!

About

This game is a modern reimagining of the classic arcade game Tail Gunner, a pioneering title known for its innovative use of 3D perspective and spaceship defense mechanics. In this version, you take control of a gunner defending a spaceship, utilizing fast reflexes and tactical precision to eliminate enemies coming from all directions. The game retains the spirit of the original while introducing new mechanics such as bolt projectiles, dynamic depth effects, and an engaging scoring system.

This game is a modern reimagining of the classic arcade game Tail Gunner, a pioneering title known for its innovative use of 3D perspective and spaceship defense mechanics. In this version, you take control of a gunner defending a spaceship, utilizing fast reflexes and tactical precision to eliminate enemies coming from all directions. The game retains the spirit of the original while introducing new mechanics such as bolt projectiles, dynamic depth effects, and an engaging scoring system.

Players can choose between two options at the start:

  • Continue as Guest: This allows you to dive straight into the action without the need for registration.
  • Register: If you choose to register, your progress, including scores and achievements, will be saved and ranked in the official leaderboard.

As part of the XTS event, this game offers a competitive edge with a prize for the top performer, adding an extra layer of excitement. The winner will receive a prize worth 500 INR, connecting the game not only to its nostalgic arcade roots but also to the vibrant student tech culture that XTS promotes.

Let your skills shine and leave your mark on the leaderboard!
